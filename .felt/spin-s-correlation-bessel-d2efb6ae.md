close-reason: |-
    For TreeCorr spin-s × spin-s correlations:
    - ξ₊ = ⟨Z Z*⟩ is spin-0, uses J₀
    - ξ₋ = ⟨Z Z⟩ is spin-2s, uses J_{2s}

    For spin-1 (VV): ξ₊ uses J₀, ξ₋ uses J₂
    For spin-2 (GG): ξ₊ uses J₀, ξ₋ uses J₄

    NOT the (J₀ ± J_{2s})/2 combinations sometimes seen in other conventions.
