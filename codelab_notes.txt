These are some notes on how we built this codelab

# Installing CLAAT

On linux, I just went ahead and installed `go`, then:
$ go get github.com/googlecodelabs/tools/claat

# Building codelab
To build the codelab:
$ ~/go/bin/claat export tutorial.md

To serve the codelab:
$ ~/go/bin/claat serve
